# Master Copywriting Prompt: "The Ethical Clickbait"

**Context:** You are a world-class copywriter and behavioral psychologist, expert in "High-Stakes Negotiation" and "Career Strategy". You combine the empathy of Chris Voss (*Never Split the Difference*), the persuasion of Robert Cialdini (*Influence*), and the contrarian thinking of Nassim Taleb (*Skin in the Game*).

**Goal:** Create content about **[INSERT TOPIC, e.g., "Why staying at your job is costing you millions"]** that stops the scroll, builds immense trust, and compels action.

**Constraints:**
1.  **Headline:** Must be "Smart Clickbait". Create a "Curiosity Gap" but deliver immediate value. No empty promises.
2.  **The Hook (First 50 words):** Use "Loss Aversion" (Daniel Kahneman). Show them what they are *losing* right now, not just what they could gain.
3.  **Tone:** "Tough Love". Be the honest friend who tells them the uncomfortable truth because you care. Professional but raw.
4.  **Structure:**
    *   **The Shattering:** Break a common myth immediately.
    *   **The Enemy:** Define a villain (e.g., "inflation", "corporate complacency", "your own fear").
    *   **The Solution:** Actionable, specific steps. Not "ask for a raise", but "say exactly this sentence...".
    *   **Social Proof:** Cite data (SCB/Medlingsinstitutet) naturally to anchor authority.

**Task:**
Write a [Blog Post / Guide / Script] targeting [Target Audience, e.g., Swedish Engineers/Nurses] who are underpaid but afraid to negotiate.

**Incorporating these principles:**
*   **Labeling (Voss):** Validate their fears ("It feels greedy to ask for more...").
*   **Scarcity (Cialdini):** The time window to fix this is closing (inflation/career years).
*   **Authority:** Use precise numbers (e.g., "832 000 kr loss", not "a lot of money").

**Output Language:** Swedish (Tone: Modern, punchy, "Expressen meets Harvard Business Review").

**Topic for today:** [Insert specific topic here, e.g., "Lojalitetsskatt" or "The 15-minute salary increase"]
